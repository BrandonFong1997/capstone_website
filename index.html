About the Product

Waking up in the morning is one of the more difficult tasks to perform in people's daily lives. A lot of the time, when given the choice, people will opt to snooze their alarm clock and just continue sleeping. Unfortunately, this can lead to grogginess in the short term and more severe health consequences in the long term. In order to avoid the temptation of snoozing and more importantly, improve an individual's overall wake-up experience, we are stepping away from the conventional alarm clock. awayke is a smart alarm clock in the form of a mat, which can only be deactivated by a user stepping on it. The mat also connects to the cloud to provide the user with sleep analytics, recommended wake-up times, and an additional incentive in the form of peer-to-peer motivation. Together, these features work cohesively to address the various needs of our users.

Get the user out of bed

A user may be woken up by their alarm clock, but they are just as likely to turn it off and go back to sleep. The chances of this occurring, however, drops a great deal once they are on their feet.

Provides an incentive to wake up

For users who begin to sleep through their familiar sounding alarms, an incentive to increase the motivation to wake is provided through our peer-to-peer wager system.

Wake the user up at the right time

Sleep can be categorized into stages, where it is ideal to be woken up while in the lighter stages. awayke aims to do this through sleep cycle tracking and wake-up time recommendations.
