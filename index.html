About the Product

Waking up in the morning is one of the more difficult tasks to perform in people's daily lives. A lot of the time, when given the choice, people will opt to snooze their alarm clock and just continue sleeping. Unfortunately, this can lead to grogginess in the short term and more severe health consequences in the long term. In order to avoid the temptation of snoozing and more importantly, improve an individual's overall wake-up experience, we are stepping away from the conventional alarm clock. awayke is a smart alarm clock in the form of a mat, which can only be deactivated by a user stepping on it. The mat also connects to the cloud to provide the user with sleep analytics and an additional incentive in the form of peer-to-peer motivation.
